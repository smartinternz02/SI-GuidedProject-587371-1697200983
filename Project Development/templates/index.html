About Project

The research, "A Comprehensive Analysis of the IT Sector's Salaries and Roles," is a thorough investigation of pay scales, employment patterns, and job functions in the Information Technology (IT) sector that is data-driven. This project entails gathering, evaluating, and presenting data from several sources to provide a thorough picture of the status of the IT industry with regard to employment trends and compensation.

The key objectives of this project include:

  • Salary Analysis
  • Role Exploration
  • Demographic Insights
